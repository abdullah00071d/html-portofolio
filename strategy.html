Marketing Strategy

Marketing Strategies
To tackle these, the business will implement a strategy centred on the 4Ps:

Product:


Offer comprehensive, customizable packages with unique Caribbean-inspired elements, meeting diverse client needs and emphasising high-quality service.

Price:


Use tiered pricing with options for different budgets, along with seasonal and referral discounts to attract and retain clients year-round.

Place:


Build a strong online presence and partner with tourism boards, travel agencies, and local venues to reach both local and international clients effectively.

Promotion:


Focus on digital marketing, influencer collaborations, and seasonal advertising to drive engagement, supported by testimonials and client referrals to build credibility.

These strategies differentiate Festivity Hub as a premium, client-centred service, enabling it to capture both local and tourist markets in the competitive Barbados event planning industry.
